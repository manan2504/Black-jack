About Game

How to play

  1. Press the Deal button to shuffle the cards and begin a new game.
  2. Press the Hit button to add a new card to your hand.
  3. End your turn and let the dealer play by pressing the Stand button.
  4. See if you managed to beat the dealer!
    Just don't go bust (score over 21) first!
